Delete your Zara account & data

Last updated: May 2026

Crowstone operates the Zara companion app. You can delete your account and associated data using either method below.

Option 1 — In the app

  1. Open Zara on your device.
  2. Go to Profile (from the chat menu).
  3. Tap Account.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This removes your session on the device and resets the app. To also delete your chat history and server-side account data, use Option 2 or email us after deleting from the app.

Option 2 — Email request

Email deepesh@crowstone.ai from the address you use for support (if any) with:

  • Subject: Zara account deletion request
  • Your Zara username (shown in Profile), if you know it
  • Confirmation that you want your account and chat data deleted

We process deletion requests within 30 days and will confirm by email when complete.

What is deleted

  • Account identifier and authentication credentials
  • Chat messages and voice note metadata stored on our servers
  • Associated analytics events linked to your account
