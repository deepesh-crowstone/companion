Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 2026

Crowstone ("we", "us") operates the Zara companion app. These standards describe our commitment to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM), on our platform.

Zero tolerance

We have zero tolerance for CSAE and CSAM. We prohibit any content, behavior, or interaction that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors. Accounts involved in such activity may be suspended or permanently removed, and we may report violations to appropriate authorities.

Age requirements

Zara is intended for adults. Users must meet the minimum age required by their local laws and the app store from which they download the app. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children in violation of applicable child-protection laws.

Prevention practices

  • AI safety filters and moderation layers designed to block sexual content involving minors and related harmful requests.
  • Ongoing review of safety signals, user reports, and platform abuse patterns to improve detection and response.
  • Restrictions on features and prompts that could facilitate grooming, exploitation, or the creation or sharing of CSAM.
  • Employee and vendor access controls so only authorized personnel can handle safety-related reviews when required.

Reporting and enforcement

If you encounter content or behavior that may involve CSAE or CSAM, report it immediately:

  • In the app: Profile → Support, or email hello@crowstone.ai.
  • Include relevant details (username, time, screenshots if available) so we can investigate quickly.

We review safety reports promptly, remove violating content where confirmed, take action against offending accounts, and preserve information as needed for legal compliance.

Cooperation with authorities

Where required by law, we report confirmed CSAM and related CSAE activity to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or other relevant regional authorities, and cooperate with lawful requests from law enforcement.

Continuous improvement

We update our safety systems, policies, and staff practices as threats and platform capabilities evolve. This page will be revised when our standards change materially.

Contact

For child safety, CSAM prevention, or compliance inquiries, contact: hello@crowstone.ai
